Who will be opening for twenty one pilots in January?
Jon Bellion was announced to be opening for some of twenty one pilots’ shows, on the 2017 tour. He will sadly not be attending the Chicago show on January 28th. However, he will be at the Moline show on the following day. It was also just announced that Judah & The Lion will be opening for twenty one pilots in 2017. This includes both the Chicago and Moline show, in January. We will keep you updated as soon as we find out who else will be opening for twenty one pilots at Chicago and Moline.
How early should I get to the venue?
If you have tickets for general admission and plan on camping, know that most venues won’t allow you to camp on their property. The venue will usually open their gates about 12 hours before the concert. But fans will plan to start an unofficial line not too far outside of the venue property, at least 24-36 hours before the concert. We will also keep you updated on when people start to line up for both #ChicagoRoadshow2 and #MolineRoadshow from our twitter account, @IllinoisClique.
What should I take to the concert?
If you have seat tickets, then you don’t have much to worry about. A camera could come in handy. Be sure to dress appropriately. Especially if the venue is outside. If you have floor tickets, then you have a bit more to plan out. It’s highly recommended you wear light weight clothing and comfortable shoes into general admission. It can get extremely hot, even if it’s cool at first. Though they aren’t the most stylish accessory, a fanny pack will be your new best friend for the night. It can easily store a lot of your belongings and are accepted into most venues. Bringing the money for what you want to buy from the merchandise tables, plus $10-20 extra, for emergencies. Bring a battery pack and charger for your phone, if you plan on camping in line. Bring a water bottle to stay hydrated during the concert. Some venues don’t allow any outside drinks, so be sure to hydrate before the concert, as well. It’s recommended you bring some band-aids and Ibuprofen, if it’s allowed. General admission is bound to have its ups and downs and changes. Things can happen, things can go wrong, people can get injured. It’s always good to be prepared. But, do not bring more than you can handle. Only bring what is necessary. And most importantly, don’t forget to take your tickets!
How do I win meet and greet tickets?
Start tuning in to your local alternative radio stations on a daily basis, a couple of months prior to the concert. For Chicago, pay attention to 101.1 WKQX and 103.5 KISS FM. They will usually give away tickets for their Chicago shows. Be sure to follow their social media accounts, as well. We will also be informing you when they're doing giveaways on our twitter page and website!
Where can I buy tickets from?
Tickets for both the Chicago and Moline shows in January are sold out. But you can buy tickets from people reselling them on websites such as StubHub, SeatGeek, and VividSeats.
When is the best time to buy tickets?
The Holidays are coming up and many people are wanting tickets from resale sites. If you aren't worried about money and want good tickets, it's recommended you consider buying tickets very soon. If you are more worried about money and dont care what seats you get, wait until after the Holidays, when the prices of tickets drop.
